What is Arcelus?

Arcelus is a Roblox exploit tool, also known as an executor. It's a type of modification for Roblox that allows you to run custom scripts in the game. With Arcelus, users can activate unofficial or unauthorized programs to modify the gameplay, often used for executing Lua scripts.
